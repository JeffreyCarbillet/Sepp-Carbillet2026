# Sepp-Carbillet2026
Dataset used for the manuscript entitled: Sex-specific effects of parental age on the physiology of offspring in a long-lived seabird

# Description of the Data and file structure
The file contains 17 columns.

NestID: Unique nest ID of the breeding pair and hatchling
ID: Unique ID (ring number) of the hatchling
MotherH: Maternal Head-bill length in mm
FatherH: Paternal Head-bill length in mm
Cortico: Baseline plasma corticosterone level of the hatchling, in ng/ml
Delay: Time delay to collect the blood sample of the hatchling, in decimal minutes
BodyMass: Hatchling mass, on the day of hatching, in grams
MotherBreeding: Maternal breeding experience, in years. Corresponds to the number of years the mother has been breeding
FatherBreeding: Paternal breeding experience, in years. Corresponds to the number of years the father has been breeding
RTL: Relative telomere length of the hatchling
Het: Heterophil count of the hatchling
Lymph: Lymphocyte count of the hatchling
Mono: Monocyte count of the hatchling
Eosino: Eosinophil count of the hatchling
Baso: Basophil count of the hatchling
growth.1.2: Growth of the hatchling over the first 24h of life, expressed in g gained or lost between the first day of life and the second one
egg.mass: Mass of the egg, at laying, from which the chick hatched, in grams
